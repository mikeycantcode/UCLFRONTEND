get started. frequently asked Q's.

Step 1: Find a counterparty

Anyone with an ethereum/arbitrum address can be your counterparty! Talk to them on your platform of choice and discuss what should happen in a default. Sign a paper contract if neccesary!

Step 2: Send a loan

The lender then sends a loan to the borrower with whatever terms have been agreed upon. Either party can cancel using the website before the loan is accepted. The lender always starts!

Step 3: Accept The Loan

Once the loan is accepted and the required collateral is supplied, the loan begins and the borrower can withdraw funds, while the lender can withdraw yield as it's paid back.

all loans and contracts are legally governed by whatever contract you have signed. please do your due diligence.
